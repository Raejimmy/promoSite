The Legend

In a medieval fantasy realm on the brink of destruction, "Warrior's Sin" follows the journey of Kalen, a disgraced knight who made a forbidden pact with dragons to save his dying daughter. The cost: his soul and servitude as their champion.

When a tyrannical emperor harnesses ancient magic to enslave the dragons and unleash them against rebelling kingdoms, Kalen finds himself caught between loyalties.

"Was his original sin an act of love or selfishness, and what price is he willing to pay to right his wrongs?"

As cities burn and the skies fill with fire-breathing terrors, Kalen discovers that his daughter, now grown, leads the resistance against the emperor. His path to redemption lies in betraying either the dragons who saved his bloodline or the daughter who never knew he survived.
